# Mirrorspine Band Creative Guidelines

## Narrative
Mirrorspine is an experimental band blending elements of post-rock and ambient soundscapes. Their music evokes imagery of introspection and cosmic exploration, inviting listeners to immerse themselves in an auditory journey.

## Preferred Instruments
- Electric Guitar
- Synthesizers
- Drums (acoustic and electronic)
- Bass Guitar
- Violin
- Ambient Field Recordings

## Mood Descriptors
- Ethereal
- Melancholic
- Uplifting
- Dreamy
- Introspective

## Production Techniques
- Use of reverb and delay to create spacious atmospheres.
- Layering of multiple instrumental tracks to build complexity.
- Incorporation of field recordings to enhance texture.
- Emphasis on dynamics, utilizing both soft passages and climactic peaks.
- Experimentation with unconventional time signatures.

---

These guidelines should serve as a foundational document to keep the band's creative direction cohesive and focused.
